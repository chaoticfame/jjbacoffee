OUR BIZARRE TALE

In the year 2025, a mysterious arrow pierced a humble coffee bean in the mountains of Ethiopia. That bean awakened with a STAND - the power to create the most extraordinary coffee in existence.

Our founder, Joe Joestar, discovered this bizarre phenomenon during his travels. He established STAND & BREW to share these awakened beans with Stand users and ordinary coffee lovers alike.

Each cup is brewed with the fighting spirit of a JoJo, the precision of a Stand battle, and the unbreakable bonds of friendship that define our bizarre adventure.

"Your next line is: 'This is the best coffee I've ever had!'"

- Joe Joestar, Founder
